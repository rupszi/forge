# Code of Conduct

Forge adopts the **Contributor Covenant v2.1** verbatim. The full text is the canonical version maintained at:

https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

Translations: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/translations/

## Scope

Applies to all project spaces — GitHub issues / PRs / Discussions, Discord, email, in-person events — and when an individual is officially representing the project in public.

## Reporting

Report concerns by opening a private email to **conduct@forge.dev** (set up at launch) or by direct-messaging a maintainer on Discord. All reports are handled confidentially. Reports are reviewed within 7 days; the reporter is updated on the outcome.

If you believe a maintainer is the source of the concern, you may instead contact a non-maintainer Forge contributor via DM.

## Enforcement

Maintainers follow the **Community Impact Guidelines** in the Contributor Covenant for determining consequences (correction → warning → temporary ban → permanent ban). The current maintainer makes the final call.
